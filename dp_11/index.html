买卖股票的最佳时机(Ⅰ,Ⅱ,Ⅲ)

kamike
2022-03-29
数据结构 / 动态规划


问题I:

Say you have an array for which the ith element is the price of a given stock on day i.

If you were only permitted to complete at most one transaction (i.e., buy one and sell one share of the stock), design an algorithm to find the maximum profit.

Note that you cannot sell a stock before you buy one.

Example 1:

Input: [7,1,5,3,6,4]
Output: 5
Explanation: Buy on day 2 (price = 1) and sell on day 5 (price = 6), profit = 6-1 = 5.
             Not 7-1 = 6, as selling price needs to be larger than buying price.

Example 2:

Input: [7,6,4,3,1]
Output: 0
Explanation: In this case, no transaction is done, i.e. max profit = 0.

题意分析:

给出一个数组,第i个元素表示第i天股票的价格,你需要在第i天买入股票并在第j天卖出(j>i,卖出必须在购买之后),要求获得的利润最大.

动态规划

方法一
我们用ai来表示数组的元素(i为数组的下标,i∈[1…n]).
固我们需要求的目标为max(aj-ai)(其中j>i).
我们用dp[i]来表示前i个数组元素的得到的利润最大值,假设最大值为ay-ax.
那么不难得出dp[i+1]=max(dp[i],ai+1-minprice),其中minprice为[0…i]中最低的价格.
那么最大的利润为dp[n-1].

方法二
以ai-ai-1重新建立新数组:
[a1-a0,a2-a1,a3-2….an-an-1]
发现需要得到aj-ai:
只需累加aj-aj-1+aj-1-j-2+…ai+1-ai，即新数组中其中一段的和.
那么问题便转化为:求数组的最大子序列的和.

问题Ⅱ:

Say you have an array for which the ith element is the price of a given stock on day i.

Design an algorithm to find the maximum profit. You may complete as many transactions as you like (i.e., buy one and sell one share of the stock multiple times).

Note: You may not engage in multiple transactions at the same time (i.e., you must sell the stock before you buy again).

Example 1:

Input: [7,1,5,3,6,4]
Output: 7
Explanation: Buy on day 2 (price = 1) and sell on day 3 (price = 5), profit = 5-1 = 4.
             Then buy on day 4 (price = 3) and sell on day 5 (price = 6), profit = 6-3 = 3.

Example 2:

Input: [1,2,3,4,5]
Output: 4
Explanation: Buy on day 1 (price = 1) and sell on day 5 (price = 5), profit = 5-1 = 4.
             Note that you cannot buy on day 1, buy on day 2 and sell them later, as you are
             engaging multiple transactions at the same time. You must sell before buying again.

Example 3:

Input: [7,6,4,3,1]
Output: 0
Explanation: In this case, no transaction is done, i.e. max profit = 0.

题意分析

与上一题不同,这题增加了要求,买卖可以进行多次

问题Ⅲ

Say you have an array for which the ith element is the price of a given stock on day i.

Design an algorithm to find the maximum profit. You may complete at most k transactions.

Note:

You may not engage in multiple transactions at the same time (ie, you must sell the stock before you buy again).

Example 1:

Input: [2,4,1], k = 2
Output: 2
Explanation: Buy on day 1 (price = 2) and sell on day 2 (price = 4), profit = 4-2 = 2.

Example 2:

Input: [3,2,6,5,0,3], k = 2
Output: 7
Explanation: Buy on day 2 (price = 2) and sell on day 3 (price = 6), profit = 6-2 = 4.
             Then buy on day 5 (price = 0) and sell on day 6 (price = 3), profit = 3-0 = 3.

题意分析

这题限制了交易次数,只能交易k次.

动态规划

有了第一题的基础,解决这题只需将数组分成k段,分别计算k段的最大值,加起来便为局部的最大值,为什么是局部最大值呢,因为分成两段有很多种情况,这些最大值只是该情况下的最优值，而不是全局的最优值.
我们用local[i][k]表示[0…i]进行k次交易,并且最后一次交易时在第i天卖出的最大值
用global[i][k]表示[0…i]进行k次交易的最大值
那么可以得出递推式:
local[i][j]=max(global[i-1][j-1]+diff,local[i-1][j]+diff)(diff表示第i天与第i-1天的价格差值,局部最优只能从j-1次交易的最大值中+价格差值和前一天的局部最优加上价格差值所产生)
global[i][j]=max(global[i-1][j],local[i][j])
